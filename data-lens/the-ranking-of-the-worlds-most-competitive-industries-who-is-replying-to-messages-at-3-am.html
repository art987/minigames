📊 Here Comes the Ranking of "Involution Kings"!

In 2025, the Global Workforce Index, in conjunction with a global workplace data platform, calculated the average working hours, night - time message response rate, and turnover rate of employees in various industries to comprehensively form the Voluntary Overwork Index (VOI). The data sample covered 27 countries and nearly 8 million people.

🌙 Who Is Still Online at 3 AM?

The Global Slack Usage Report shows that the top five industries with the highest message density from 23:00 to 04:00 are:

  • Game industry: The proportion of active users at night reaches 34%.
  • Media industry: Editors are on standby 24 hours a day.
  • E - commerce operations: It is normal to replenish orders and adjust inventory in the early morning.
  • Creative advertising: Inspiration never punches in and out.
  • Consulting industry: Synchronizing with global clients, night - shift meetings have become the norm.
